Christian Jones

Duke University

christian.jones@duke.edu

Christian M. Jones is a second-year Duke Sanford School of Public Policy student in the Master of National Security Policy program. She joins the SRCUACS community as the project manager for the Southeast Region. Her goal is to increase engagement across the Region by promoting the six elements of SRCUACS and creating a space for community to be felt across digital platforms.

Her academic interests include cybersecurity policy and national challenges in the areas of education and workforce talent. A native of South New Jersey, Jones graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Political Science from Saint Augustine’s University where she served as a student member of the board of trustees. Prior to her time at Sanford, she spent time as a legal assistant in the New York City Metropolitan Area, an AmeriCorps member, and administrative coordinator for various academic projects. Passionate about fostering children's confidence and inspiring policy initiatives for globally competitive leaders, Jones aims to leverage her talents in the national security environment by integrating education, workforce development, and cybersecurity policy.

During her breaks from academic pursuits, she nourishes her soul by attending spin class; cooking and baking for others with love; teaching children the art of etiquette; and embracing the richness of new languages and cultures.
