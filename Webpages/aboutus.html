About

The Southeast Regional Coalition for University-Assisted Community Schools (SRCUACS) was recently awarded as the 6th regional technical training center for university-assisted community schools (UACS) by the Netter Center for Community Engagement at the University of Pennsylvania.

SRCUACS will serve the critically important role of coalescing colleges and universities within the state and across the region to enhance practice and advance policy change for the expansion and sustainability of community schools. SRCUACS will act as a convener and central hub organized around six pillars: Coalition Building, Storytelling, Connecting, Pipeline Development, Policy Change, and Research.
