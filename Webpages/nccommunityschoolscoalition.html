North Carolina Community Schools Coalition (NCCSC)

Our Mission

The North Carolina Community School Coalition (NCCSC), guided by the Whole School, Whole Community, Whole Child Framework and the four pillars of community schools, aims to improve the academic, mental, and physical health of North Carolina through three strategic priorities:

The integration of cross-sector partnerships and programming in NC schools guided by the shared leadership of students, guardians, teachers, and staff.
The creation of three Implementation Partners located initially in Eastern, Central and Western NC. Regional Implementation Partners will support school districts and individual schools in implementing the Community School model while expanding the impact and reach of community schools within the area.
State Implementation Partners will increase capacity of each Regional Implementation Partner, facilitate cross region learning, and support statewide educational equity efforts.
